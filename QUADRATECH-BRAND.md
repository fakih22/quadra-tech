## Writing Style

### Do's ✓
- Use active voice
- Be specific and concrete
- Focus on client benefits
- Include measurable results
- Use technical terms appropriately
- Write clear, scannable content

### Don'ts ✗
- Avoid buzzwords without substance
- Don't oversell or use hyperbole
- Don't be overly technical with non-technical audiences
- Avoid jargon when simpler terms work
- Don't make claims you can't back up
